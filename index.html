✨ School Motto

We believe that great success is built on small, consistent actions — integrity, kindness, and diligence in every detail.

🔭 Vision

To be a school of excellence in developing all students academically,socially, emotionally and spiritually to compete and contribute in a global society.

💎 Core Values

  • Integrity – We all live as proof of moral principles
  • Respect – We treat everyone with courtesy and honour
  • Commitment –We show commitment to our students
  • Responsibility – We are trustworthy, reliable and accountable
  • High Expectation – We all set high goals, inspire personalbest and continuousimprovementof all staff and studentsS

  • Academic Excellence
