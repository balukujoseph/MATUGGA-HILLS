CURRICULUM

The school provides a rich and diverse curriculum that is fully aligned with Uganda’s New Lower Secondary Curriculum. Through a learner-centered approach, we empower students with relevant knowledge, practical skills, values, and competencies that enable them to confidently face the challenges and opportunities of the modern world. By offering a broad range of subjects and learning experiences, the school promotes the development of independent thinkers, creative problem-solvers, and responsible citizens. Our curriculum goes beyond academic excellence by nurturing innovation, talent, leadership, and practical abilities, preparing learners for higher education, meaningful careers, and lifelong success in a rapidly changing global environment.
